Join the Chamber

Membership Levels

NP Membership:

Free membership for non-profit organizations. Includes access to community networking events and basic training sessions.

Gold Membership:

Premium level with the most benefits, including exclusive advertising opportunities, spotlight positions on the homepage, VIP event access, free training sessions, and enhanced marketing support.

Silver Membership:

Provides additional perks such as priority event registration, extended training workshops, marketing support, and a featured listing on our website.

Bronze Membership:

Affordable entry-level membership that includes event discounts, access to networking meetups, and periodic training opportunities.
